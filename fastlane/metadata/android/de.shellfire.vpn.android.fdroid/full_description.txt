ShellfireVPN-Android is an open-source Android application that provides a secure and private VPN connection.
This VPN client can **only be used** to connect to the **Shellfire VPN network**.

Features:
* Secure VPN connection with encryption
* Open-source and privacy-focused
* Two build flavors:
  * **googlePlay:** For distribution on the Google Play Store (with proprietary dependencies)
  * **fdroid:** Fully free and open-source build for F-Droid
* Uses modern Gradle build flavors and stubs to separate proprietary dependencies

The F-Droid version is completely **free of proprietary dependencies** and follows FLOSS principles.
